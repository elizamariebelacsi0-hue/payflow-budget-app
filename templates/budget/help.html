How to use Payflow

Payflow is your personal budget management and bill reminder app. Here's how to get started:

1. Adding Categories

Create payment categories for your bills like rent, utilities, internet, etc. Each category includes the amount and due date.

2. Recording Payments

When you make a payment, record it in the category detail page. This helps track your payment history.

3. Setting Budget

Set your monthly budget to track your spending and stay within your financial limits.

4. Notifications

The app will notify you 1-2 days before payments are due to help you stay on top of your bills.

Features
  • Budget tracking and management
  • Bill reminders and notifications
  • Payment history and transaction records
  • Category-based organization
  • Personal profile management
